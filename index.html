Our Story

Born from a love
of beautiful craft

Raga Designers was founded with a singular conviction — that design isn't decoration, it's the language a brand uses to speak to the world. Over eight years, we've partnered with startups, enterprises, and cultural institutions to give their ideas a form worthy of their ambition.

We're a small, intentional team. We take fewer projects than we could — so each one receives the attention it deserves. Every pixel, every word, every interaction is a deliberate choice.
